Thakur College of Engineering & Technology

Welcome to the Department of Computer Engineering (CMPN). Permanently affiliated to the University of Mumbai and NBA accredited.

General Profile

Established: Academic Year 2001-02

Affiliation: Permanently affiliated to University of Mumbai (since AY 2015-16)

Accreditation: UG program is NBA accredited.

Location: A-Block, Thakur Educational Campus, Shyamnarayan Thakur Marg, Thakur Village, Kandivali (E), Mumbai – 400101

Academics & Infrastructure

Intake & Programs

  • UG (B.E. in Computer Engineering): 180 seats
  • Lateral Entry (Diploma → 2nd Year): 20 seats
  • PG (M.E. in Computer Engineering): 18 seats (since AY 2011-12)
  • Ph.D. Research Centre available

Infrastructure & Facilities

  • Faculty strength: ~26 members
  • Labs & Classrooms: 6 UG Labs + 1 PG Lab + Research Centre (Ph.D.)
  • Classrooms: 8 UG Classrooms (5 online/TLP enabled) + 1 PG Classroom + 1 Smart Classroom
  • Other Facilities: 4 Seminar Halls, Multipurpose Room, Digital Library

Curriculum & Subjects

Core Engineering

Data Structures, Digital Logic Design, DBMS, Operating Systems, Microprocessors, Computer Networks, DSP, Image Processing, Theory of CS, OOP Methodology

Advanced Topics

Advanced DBMS, Advanced Microprocessor, Advanced Computer Networks, Software Engineering, Intelligent Systems, Cryptography & Security, Embedded Systems, Parallel & Distributed Computing

Application Areas

Web Engineering, Computer Graphics, Computer Vision, AI, Mobile Computing, Human-Computer Interaction, Digital Forensics, Big Data Analytics, Distributed Databases, E-Commerce
